Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to complete a custom painting?
Most standard portraits take 7-10 days to complete. Complex pieces or multiple subjects may require 2-3 weeks. We'll provide a timeline when you place your order.
What's your payment policy?
All orders require prepayment before we begin work. We accept payments via UPI, bank transfer, and other digital methods. Our customer care team will contact you with payment details.
Can I request changes after the painting has started?
Minor adjustments can be made during the initial sketch phase. Once color application begins, changes may be limited. We'll share progress photos for your approval at key stages.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we primarily serve customers within India. For international orders, please contact us directly to discuss shipping options and additional costs.
What materials do you use for paintings?
We use high-quality art supplies including professional-grade pencils, watercolors, and acrylics on premium art paper or canvas. Each piece is carefully finished to ensure longevity.
How do I reference a specific photo from your gallery?
When contacting us about artwork from our gallery, please use the photo reference numbers provided on this page. This helps us quickly identify the specific piece you're interested in.
